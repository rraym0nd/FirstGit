About

This page is dedicated to all things about DDOS.

DDOS is an evasive platformer game by Retr01 about redlining, digital futures, and the evolution of money technology as we know it.

It is inspired by Art Deco, 8 Bit art, Anime, Prince of Persia, and American Redlining practices that began after WWII, and are still evident today.

Besides the storyline and aesthetics of this game, unique aspects include the parkour movement that is utilized as a mechanic, reminiscent of Mirror's Edge.

Important Notes:

  • Platform: Web
  • Rating: E for Everyone, as defined by the ESRB
  • Launch Date: June 10th, 2019
  • Playtest Dates: 5/23, 5/30, 6/3
